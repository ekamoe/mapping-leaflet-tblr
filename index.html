About The Bare Life Review

The Bare Life Review is the only literary journal whose sole mission is to publish immigrant and refugee writers. Contributors are either foreign-born and living in the United States, or are living abroad holding refugee and/or asylum-seeker status.

Map

This map displays the birthplaces of contributors to the inaugural issue of The Bare Life Review. Click the icons to learn more about the writers and editors.

Notes
